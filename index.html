FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use it?
No. ShutterSmart Pro is designed for tradespeople, not software engineers. If you can use a smartphone, you can use this. Most installers are set up and quoting within an hour.
Can I try it before paying?
Yes — 7 days completely free, no credit card required. If it's not right for you, just don't subscribe. No awkward calls or cancellation hoops.
Does it work on my phone on site?
Yes. ShutterSmart Pro is fully responsive and works on any smartphone, tablet or desktop. Build quotes, take photos and add survey notes straight from the customer's home.
Does it handle blinds as well as shutters?
Both. There's a dedicated blind section alongside shutters, with its own supplier and pricing setup. Mixed quotes with shutters and blinds on the same job are fully supported.
Can I add multiple suppliers with different pricing?
Yes. You can add as many suppliers as you like, each with their own cost prices and margins. When quoting, just select the supplier and the pricing calculates automatically.
How does lead source tracking work?
When you add a customer you can tag where they came from — Google, referral, Instagram, Checkatrade, leaflet drop, or anything custom. Over time ShutterSmart Pro shows you which sources bring the most quotes, the best conversion rates and the highest revenue, so you know where to spend your marketing budget.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel any time from your account. You won't be charged again and keep access until the end of your billing period. Your data is yours — export it any time.
